Subtract $100 from your collision deductible after each year without an accident. Your collision deductible decreases by $100 immediately and never resets, even if you have an accident. Collision deductibles can decrease a maximum of $500.

To be eligible for Diminishing Deductible, all vehicles with collision coverage must have a deductible of $500 or more. Vehicles that have no collision coverage do not prevent eligibility on other vehicles. If Diminishing Deductible is selected on one vehicle it will be required on all eligible vehicles.
