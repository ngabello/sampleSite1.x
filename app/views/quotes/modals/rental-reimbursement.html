Rental Reimbursement

Rental Reimbursement pays up to a preset amount for a rental vehicle if your car isn't drivable for more than 24 hours because of a covered accident. Rental Reimbursement helps to reduce the hassle of an accident by providing for a replacement car, while your car is in the repair shop, or if your car was a total loss, until you have purchased another car, subject to the coverage limits.

Your coverage limits, deductibles, and certain exclusions may apply. Please read your Elephant Insurance Company policy for details
