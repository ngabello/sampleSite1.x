What is Property Damage Liability?

In an accident where you / driver of your car are at fault, you are covered for property damage of another individual that results from the accident. Damage to property also includes damage that you cause to public property such as sign posts, telephone poles and fences.

Your coverage limits, deductibles, and certain exclusions may apply. Please read your Elephant Insurance Company policy for details.

How Much Coverage Do I Need

Individual states have different minimum requirements for Property Damage Liability insurance. In Illinois you are required to have a minimum of $15,000. If you lease a car, your lease contract will typically require you to maintain minimum Property Damage Liability Coverage of $50,000. You should buy enough Property Damage Liability coverage to adequately protect your assets.
