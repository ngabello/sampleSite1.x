What Is Uninsured Motorist Property Damage?

Uninsured Motorist Property Damage pays for damage to your vehicle caused by an uninsured driver. If this coverage applies in an accident, you will be required to pay a $250 deductible. These coverages only apply if it is determined that the other driver is at fault and has no liability coverage. Your coverage limits, deductibles, and certain exclusions may apply. What Is Uninsured Motorist Property Damage?

Uninsured Motorist Property Damage pays for damage to your vehicle caused by an uninsured driver or in many cases, a hit-and-run driver. If this coverage applies in an accident, you will be required to pay a $250 deductible when the uninsured motorist cannot be identified. The company pays for additional costs covered under the policy.

These coverages only apply if it is determined that the other driver is at fault and has insufficient or no liability coverage.

Your coverage limits, deductibles, and certain exclusions may apply. Please read your Elephant Insurance Company policy for details.
