What Is Medical Payments Coverage?

Medical Payments Benefits provide coverage for medical expenses resulting from bodily injury caused by an accident and arising out of the ownership, maintenance or use of a motor vehicle as a motor vehicle.

Coverage is also provided to the insured and resident relatives, while they are riding in someone else's car at the time of the accident or if they are struck as a pedestrian.

Your coverage limits, deductibles, and certain exclusions may apply. Please read your Elephant Insurance Company policy for details.
