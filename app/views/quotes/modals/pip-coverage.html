Personal Injury Protection ("PIP") coverage protects you and persons related to you and living in your home for injuries sustained in any motor vehicle accident, anyone injured while in your insured vehicle with your permission and pedestrians injured by your insured vehicle. PIP protects you while in your own or someone else's vehicle; however, there is no coverage if the car you are riding in is owned by you or a resident relative and it is not insured. It also protects you as a pedestrian or bicyclist if you suffer an injury in an accident involving a motor vehicle. Your coverage limits, deductibles, and certain exclusions may apply. Please read your Elephant Insurance policy for details.
